How to Bake a Cake

Baking a cake can not only be fun but also very, very yummy. Using the listed ingredients and instructions, you can be well on your way to a delicious treat.

Listed are the following ingredients you will need...

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
Directions
Step 1 Preheat oven to 350F
Step 2 Butter the cakepans
Step 3 Wisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl
Step 4 Beat two sticks of butter, sugar, and eggs in bowl
Step 5 Combine both bowls and wisk until smooth
Step 6 Bake in oven for 30-35 minutes
Step 7 Let cake COOL for 10 minutes
Step 8 Add Frosting and ENJOY!

After following these directions and using the ingredients provided, the result will be a delicious cake. When baking, remember to use the right amount of ingredients and to follow the down to the last step. As Elon Musk said, Building a company can be a lot like baking a cake. You must have the right ingredients in the right proportions.
